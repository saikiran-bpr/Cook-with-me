Pre-Preparation

Process for soaking Paneer

In a bowl add paneer and soak it in warm salted water and keep aside for further use.

Process for making curd mixture

In a bowl add degi red-chilli powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, curd, 2 slices of butter and mix everything properly and keep it aside for further use.
